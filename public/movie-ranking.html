The Best Movies According to Rachille

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Invisible Guest

A gripping Spanish thriller directed by Oriol Paulo, The Invisible Guest revolves around a successful businessman who is accused of murder and enlists the help of a skilled defense attorney. The film is filled with twists and turns, keeping the audience constantly guessing as it weaves a complex narrative of lies, deceit, and manipulation. Its clever storytelling and tight pacing make it an intense and engaging watch.

Drishyam 1 & 2

These Indian crime thrillers, directed by Jeethu Joseph, follow the story of Georgekutty, a simple man who goes to extraordinary lengths to protect his family when they become entangled in a criminal investigation. In both Drishyam films, Georgekutty uses his intelligence and knowledge of movies to outsmart the police and cover up the truth. The sequel further deepens the tension as his past comes back to haunt him, exploring themes of justice, family loyalty, and the consequences of one's actions.

Knives Out

Directed by Rian Johnson, Knives Out is a modern take on the classic whodunit genre. The film centers around the mysterious death of a wealthy crime novelist, and the investigation that follows, led by the eccentric detective Benoit Blanc. With a stellar ensemble cast, clever dialogue, and multiple plot twists, Knives Out keeps the audience on the edge of their seats while also delivering sharp social commentary and a satisfying, intricately woven mystery.
