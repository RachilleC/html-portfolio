About Me

I worked there for a year before I became a Branch Manager at a well-known remittance center and Pawnshop here in the Philippines. It took me four years before I decided to resign to look for a job online.

After a few months since I resigned from my last job, I took an online course in full-stack development and here I am starting to pursue my dream job, to become a web developer. Ever since I graduated, I really dreamed of finding a job related to the course I completed, but that was not easy and I ended up working in various industries

Anyway, this is still one of the advantages I look at because I had different experiences in other jobs that are not related with what I studied and I know that I can still use those experiences in whatever job I have in the future. I'm still far from my dream job but I know I can get there and I just have to trust the process and of course my own abilities.
